#1 Image optimization and Compression

To save some time and not have a slower page, compressing your files and images may go a long way. For the image I used in assignment 1, the website I used was Compresspng.com. It helped compress and optimize a pretty medium sized png photo by 67%. Meaning the height and with of new photo is two thirds less big and cluttered.
